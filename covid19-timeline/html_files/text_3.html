January 31, 2020
On Jan. 31, the United States suspended entry for people who had recently been to China with the exception of immediate family members of American citizens or permanent residents.
Italy becomes the first state in the European Union to declare a state of emergency.
Several other countries have already closed the borders to all visitors arriving from mainland China.
If we look at the number of confirmed cases for the month of January, we can see that the number of infections outside China grew at nearly the same rate as the number of infections in the country.
In China the number of cases increased by 2.070% compared to 2.000% in the rest of the world.

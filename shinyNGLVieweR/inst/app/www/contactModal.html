Expressions
  • all residues: *
  • residue number: 1, 2, 100
  • residue number range: 3-40 (Note that around the dash - no spaces are allowed)
  • sequence: <NGLSD>
  • chain name: :A
  • atom name: .CA, .C, .N, ...
  • residue name: ALA, GLU, SOL, DMPC, ...
  • model: /0, /1, ...
Examples
  • Contacts between all residues
    • Selection A: *
    • Selection B: *
  • Contacts between chain A and chain B
    • Selection A: :A
    • Selection B: :B
  • Contacts between Biotin and chain A but not residue 128
    • Selection A: <BTN>
    • Selection B: :A and not 128
  • Contacts between chain A residues 109 or 123 and chain B residue 107
    • Selection A: :A and (109 or 123)
    • Selection B: :B and 107
See the NGL Manual for a complete overview.

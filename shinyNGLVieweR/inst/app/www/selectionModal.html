Expressions
  • residue number: 1, 2, 100
  • residue number range: 3-40 (Note that around the dash - no spaces are allowed)
  • sequence: <NGLSD>
  • chain name: :A
  • atom name: .CA, .C, .N, ...
  • residue name: ALA, GLU, SOL, DMPC, ...
  • model: /0, /1, ...
Examples
  • 1-20 #select residues 1-20
  • ALA #select all Alanine residues
  • 20-30 or <NGLSD> #select residues 20-30 and sequence "NGLSD"
  • :A and ( 1 or 10 or 100 ) #select residues 1, 10 and 100 from chain A:
  • :A and 63 and .CA #select atom .CA on residue 63 from chain A:
  • (1 OR 2 OR 3) AND NOT [HOH] #select residues 1, 2 and 3 but not [HOH]
See the NGL Manual for a complete overview.
